About Blue Cup

A forum where
ideas collide.

As AI reshapes decision-making and polarization erodes consensus, meaningful dialogue is increasingly rare. Blue Cup creates the platform that's been missing — one that treats critical thinking as a bridge and disagreement as an opportunity for clarity.

Focused on the DMV region's intersection of policy, technology, and commerce, we bring together students from universities across Washington D.C., Maryland, and Virginia.

Debate Format

How the debate
unfolds.

Each match features structured constructive speeches, cross-examination, an open free debate, and final rebuttals — giving every debater multiple opportunities to argue their case and challenge opponents.

AFF
Affirmative Side
Argues in support of the motion with constructives and final rebuttals
NEG
Negative Side
Challenges the motion through counter-arguments and cross-examination

Sides determined by draw. Full rules in the Debater's Handbook upon registration. Motions co-designed with invited scholars around frontier business topics.

Organizers

Who's behind
Blue Cup.

Host
Sino-American Commerce Association (SACA)

A registered student organization at JHU Carey Business School, founded in 2023. Integrates cross-cultural exchange and hands-on business practice with a focus on innovation & entrepreneurship.

VentureNest @ JHU

A non-profit founded by JHU alumni and students with 200+ registered members. Incubated 10+ startups across AI, health tech, and consumer products, with a strategic partnership with the JHU PAVA Center for Entrepreneurship.

Co-Host

A U.S.-registered non-profit co-built by students and alumni across North America, with the mission of "connecting people and linking campuses with cities."
